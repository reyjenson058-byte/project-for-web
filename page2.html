Off Page Seo

Off-Page SEO involves actions taken outside of the website to improve its authority, trustworthiness, and ranking in search engine results. The most important factor in Off-Page SEO is backlinks, which are links from other websites pointing to your site. Search engines often view backlinks as a signal that your content is valuable and credible.

Other Off-Page SEO strategies include social media sharing, influencer mentions, guest blogging, and online reputation management. These activities help increase brand awareness and drive traffic to the website even though they do not directly change the site’s internal structure or content.
